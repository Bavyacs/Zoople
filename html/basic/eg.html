Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

 - 1 cup unsalted butter,softened
 - 1 cup white sugar
 - 1 cup browm sugar
 - 2 large eggs
 - 2 tsp vanilla extract
 - 3 cups all-purpose flour
 - 1 tsp baking soda
 - 1/2 tsp salt
 - 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

 1. Preheat your oven to 350oF (175oC)
 2. In a large bowl,cream together the butter,White sugar,and brown sugar until smooth.
 3. Beat in the eggs one at a time,then stir in thevanilla extract. 
 4. Combine flour,baking soda,and salt → Actuallycombine the flour,baking soda,and saltin a seperate bowl,then gradually.
 5. Fold in the chocolate chips(use your favorite brand).
 6. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto ungreased baking sheet(for extra crispiness).
 7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until edges are golden brown(check every 2 mins for perfection).
 8. Allow cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transfering to a wire rack.
