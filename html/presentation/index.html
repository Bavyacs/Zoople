The Creator of HTML

Who Created HTML?

HTML was created by Tim Berners-Lee in 1991 while working at CERN.
He developed HTML to help researchers share documents through the internet.
His invention became the foundation of the World Wide Web.

About Tim Berners-Lee

  • Profession: Computer Scientist
